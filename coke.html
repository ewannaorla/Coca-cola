BOTTLING:

Coca-cola is investing heavily in bottling operations in order to maximize the strength and production,distribution and marketing.The company has three criteria for making a bottling investment:

  1. The company needs to move quickly in an emerging market
  2. When an existing bottler lacks the resources to meet the company's objective
  3. To help ensure long-term strategic alignment with key bottling partners
